# Planner Memory

- Demo history covers 90 archived days from 2026-02-24 through 2026-05-24, with realistic variability: no-completion days, weekend shrinkage, and uneven P2/P3 follow-through.
- Best daily shape: one concrete data-owner P1 artifact, one infrastructure or evaluation support task, and two lightweight optional side-interest tasks.
- The highest-completion tasks name the exact metric, source table, quality check, decision owner, or failure mode. Broad reading and vague side projects slip.
- Infrastructure learning works best as a 6-bullet decision brief: demand assumption, bottleneck, symptom, product impact, mitigation, next measurement.
- Robotics tasks should stay small: one sensor, one threshold, one actuator response, one fail-safe, and one logged-field checklist.
- Japanese is a side habit and should stay around 15 minutes on normal days.
